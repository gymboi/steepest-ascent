Background Information

Steepest Ascent or descent method, also known as the saddle-point method, is a numerical method that is an extension of Laplace's method. Mathematicians and historians accredited this method to the physicist Peter Debye in 1909. Still, there is no proven creator, for too many present articles show that the technique was used before 1909.

This method aims to find the minimum and maximum of differentiable functions. This approach or strategy's ideology is that if the gradient or slope is not zero where you are, you must move or select a direction opposite that is a gradient.


Algorithm

Step 1: Declare the values of x0 and y0.

Step 2: Given a function, get the value of the partial derivatives of x and y.

Step 3: Substitute the bisolved values of x and y with the given function and solve.

Step 4: Declare g(h) which holds the value of the solved function.

Step 5: Get the derivation of g(h) using the formula:d/dx x^n = nx^{n-1} (Power rule)

Step 6: Using g’(h), get the value of h.

Step 7: Go back to step 4 and solve for xn and yn


Limitations

Limitations of the method:

  • The steepest ascent method is a simple and intuitive optimization technique, and was also used to develop the theory of optimization. Unfortunately, this method is not widely used due to its poor and slow performance in most real-world problems (Meza, 2010). Using this method can be computationally expensive to compute the gradient at each iteration, especially for large and complex problems. This can make the method inefficient and slow to converge on a solution, therefore, it may not always find the absolute best solution to a problem.
  • Choosing the right initial point is also important for this method because it is very sensitive to initial conditions, just like in Newton’s Method (Foote, 2007). The algorithm may converge to different solutions depending on the starting point. This can make it difficult to predict the behavior of the algorithm and can lead to inconsistent results.
  • While the steepest ascent method is a useful optimization technique in some cases, it has limitations that can make it less effective for certain problems. It is important to carefully consider the strengths and weaknesses of this method and to choose the appropriate optimization algorithm for a given problem.
